Moving on from This Project!

It's been a while huh? A lot of stuff has happened, and to be honest, it does not feel like 4 weeks have gone by. I felt like I really was just trying to survive my exams. Because of that, I took more and more time away from making this game, and after a while, I realized that I lost passion.

There are a lot of reasons why I lost passion, but I would definitely say the biggest cause was a lack of preparation on my part. I didn't understand what I was getting myself into, since I believed that I could just code everything into existence. However navigating Unity's menus and getting used to all their coding conventions and libraries was something I was not prepared for.

As I progressed further into the project, I realized that I got too inundated with having to learn a whole new system for each feature I wanted to implement. I was tired of it. So I decided to start something new.

For more information about it, check out this link.

I know that only I am to blame for dropping this project, but in the end I'm alright with that, as long as I learned something new. Thank you for reading this, and have a good day.

~Osmond "TheOneAndOlee" Lee
