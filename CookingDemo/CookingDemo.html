Trying Out a Cooking System

After losing motivation with my Space Invaders Demo, I decided to think about what I actually wanted to make.

From there, I realized that my favorite game mechanic is putting any ingredients you wanted into a pot/pan and getting a beneficial food output. Some games with this style of cooking include:

  • Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild
  • Don't Starve Together
  • Super Paper Mario

Knowing that this is the game mechanic I was most interested in, I'm deciding to work on a demo for this cooking system.
